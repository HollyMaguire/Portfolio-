At Casa De Delicious we have great food with fair prices. We specilize in greasy meats, we do have salad too though. We pride ourselves on our fun, family environment. We feed the leftovers to our mascot Sir Oinkers, so nothing goes to waste. He stays out back, guest can take photos with him and he is great with kids! Where else could you go and eat sausage and then turn around and play with a pig and feed him your left overs, nowhere that is where. So eat and have fun at Casa De Delicious, a restaurant unlike any other.
